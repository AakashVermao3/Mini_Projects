Frequently Asked Questions

  • Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single commercial – all for one low monthly price. There's always something new to discover, and new TV shows and movies are added every week!

  • Netflix offers a range of subscription plans to fit your needs. Your plan will determine how many people can stream Netflix content at once, and whether you can view in Standard Definition (SD), High Definition (HD), or Ultra High Definition (UHD). For the latest pricing and plans, visit the Netflix website.

  • You can watch Netflix on any internet-connected device that offers the Netflix app, such as smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, set-top boxes, smartphones, and tablets. You can also stream Netflix on your computer using an internet browser.

  • Cancelling your Netflix subscription is easy. Simply sign in to Netflix and visit your Account page. Under the Membership & Billing section, click on the Cancel Membership button and follow the prompts to confirm. Once you've completed the cancellation process, you'll continue to have access to Netflix until your current billing period ends.

  • Netflix offers a vast library of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more across a wide range of genres. From popular original series like Stranger Things, The Crown, and Bridgerton to blockbuster movies, critically acclaimed documentaries, and exclusive stand-up comedy specials, there's something for everyone to enjoy on Netflix.

  • Netflix offers a dedicated Kids profile that provides a safe and ad-free environment for children to explore and enjoy age-appropriate content. Parents can set parental controls to restrict access to certain types of content and ensure that their children have a safe and enjoyable viewing experience on Netflix.
